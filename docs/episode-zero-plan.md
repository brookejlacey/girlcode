# Girl Code - Episode Zero Plan

## Concept: "We built our business in a day. Here's how."

Unmarketed trial run to test the process and capture "day one" content.

---

## Structure

### 1. The Origin Story (10-15 min)
- How Brooke and Nicki know each other, the friendship-to-business moment
- The "we have 45 years of experience between us and people keep offering us equity" frustration that sparked Girl Code
- Keep it conversational, not scripted — that's what makes it watchable

### 2. The Receipts (10-15 min)
- Pull up girlcode.technology live on stream
- Walk through what was built and why: the intake form, the equity auto-filter, the three pillars (Consulting, Development, Media)
- This is the unfair advantage — not talking about building, showing what's already shipped

### 3. What's Coming (5-10 min)
- Tease Jayla, Meridian (Avalanche hackathon), the consulting arm
- Tell people what Thursdays are going to look like going forward
- Soft CTA: "if you need a fractional CTO, the form is live right now"

---

## Why This Works for Episode Zero

- Low pressure — just telling your own story
- You have something real to show (not vaporware)
- Tests your dynamic together without needing a structured format yet
- Even if 5 people watch, you've got a clip of "day one" that becomes valuable later
- No guest prep, no research, just you two being you

---

## Logistics

- Platform: Restream (multi-stream to X, LinkedIn, TikTok)
- Day: Thursday (tentative)
- Format: Unmarketed trial run — "episode zero" to work out the flow
- Brooke handles streaming account setup
- Nicki handles Notion project management flow
